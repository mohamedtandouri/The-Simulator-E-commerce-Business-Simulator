## Overview

**The Simulator** is a comprehensive e-commerce business simulation tool that helps entrepreneurs calculate profitability metrics and make data-driven decisions for their online businesses. With its intuitive interface and accurate calculations based on real market data, you can model different business scenarios before launching your store.
